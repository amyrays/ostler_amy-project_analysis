Site Intent

Spring Lake Elementary is an A+ public school utilizing top-notch technologies. The schools current site struggles to communicate important messages and dates effectively. Spring Lake Elementary has asked that I, Amy Ostler, design and develop an up-to-date website that is functional and easy to use.

By reducing the current 21 pages of content to only 6 pages of content, reorganizing and restructuring the content, designing and developing to meet W3C standards, and using proper Information Architecture we can increase load times and gain our targeted audiences attention.

Competitive Analysis

Spring Lake Elementary (K12.fl.us) is ranked #10,826 globally according to Alexa.com with a US traffic rank of 1,874. Visitors spend approximately four minutes on this site with 35 seconds spent on each page. In comparison with the competitor site, Spring Lake Elementary ranks higher both globally and nationally, while the demographics remain the same.

Target Audience

Suzi is a 40-year-old college student with 3 children in the first, second, and sixth grades. She visits the school website primarily from home to contact the school, her children’s teachers, and keep updated with the schools current events/agenda.

Most parents accessing the site lead busy lives and need to be able to find and access information on events, school policy, and dress code quickly. Also, to be able to stay in contact with their children’s teachers and other staff through email, phone, or fax.
